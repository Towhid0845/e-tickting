Trips email sync

Now you can automatically import your booking confirmations into new or existing trips – no forwarding required. Trips Email Sync will do all the heavy lifting for you.

Supports gmail.com, outlook.com, hotmail.com, msn.com, live.com, passport.com

KAYAK's use or transfer of information received from Gmail sync is subject to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

  • Simple

    When you enable Trips Email Sync, we'll scan your incoming emails for booking confirmations and automatically add those details to new or existing Trips. Simple as that.

  • Secure

    We've integrated high-security safeguards for Email Sync. Your booking receipts will be safely stored in our systems.

  • Safe

    And don't worry, we'll only access the emails that we know are from senders used for booking confirmations. Your other emails are left untouched.
